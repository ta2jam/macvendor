# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes:

- respecting different viewpoints and technical experience;
- giving and accepting specific, evidence-based feedback;
- taking responsibility for mistakes;
- focusing review on the change, risk, and evidence rather than the person;
- protecting private, security, and licensing information.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, insults, trolling, or discriminatory language;
- publishing another person's private information without permission;
- deliberately submitting copied or misrepresented data;
- pressuring maintainers or contributors to bypass security, privacy, licensing,
  review, or verification requirements;
- conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate professionally.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests,
and other contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations
may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's
[security advisory form](https://github.com/ta2jam/macvendor/security/advisories/new)
with `[Code of Conduct]` in the title. Reports will be handled confidentially.
